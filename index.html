Bored?

Kick ass

Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar or a tab and start having some fun on other pages too!

Tip: It works great for vaporizing annoying ads. Try it out!

How to

  • Steer with the arrow-keys.
  • Shoot with space.
  • To activate click the bookmark once on your webpage of choice.
  • Can't see your remaining enemies? Then press and hold B

Remember: It's cooler if you make your own sound effects.
